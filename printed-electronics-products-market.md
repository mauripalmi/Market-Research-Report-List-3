<p><p>Printed electronics products can be categorized into different types based on the printing technology used. Inkjet printing is suitable for small scale, high resolution printing. Gravure printing offers high quality, consistent results for large scale production. Screen printing is versatile and can be used on a variety of substrates. Flexography is commonly used for packaging and labeling applications.
