<p><p>Chemotherapy Induced Nausea and Vomiting (CINV) Drugs market includes 5-HT3 Inhibitors and NK1 Inhibitors. 5-HT3 Inhibitors, such as ondansetron and granisetron, work by blocking serotonin receptors in the gut and brain to prevent nausea and vomiting. On the other hand, NK1 Inhibitors, like aprepitant and fosaprepitant, target neurokinin receptors in the brain to further reduce the risk of CINV. Both types of drugs are crucial in managing the side effects of chemotherapy and improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
