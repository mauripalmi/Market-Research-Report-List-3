<p><ul><li>Stylus Surface Profiler</li><li>Non-contact Profiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface profile measurement market consists of two main types: Stylus Surface Profilers and Non-contact Profilers. Stylus Surface Profilers utilize a mechanical stylus to measure surface roughness and texture, providing accurate and reliable results. Non-contact Profilers, on the other hand, use optical or laser technology to precisely measure surface profiles without physically touching the surface. Both types of profilers offer unique advantages and cater to different industry needs, such as manufacturing, research, and quality control.</p></p>
