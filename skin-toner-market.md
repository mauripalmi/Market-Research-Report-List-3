<p><p>Skin toner market includes various types such as skin bracers or fresheners, skin tonics, and astringents. Skin tonics are designed to improve skin tone and texture, often containing moisturizing ingredients like glycerin or vitamin E. Astringents are more drying and are used to reduce oiliness and minimize pores. Each type of toner caters to different skin concerns and preferences.</p></p>
