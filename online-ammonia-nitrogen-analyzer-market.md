<p><strong>The Online Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE)</li><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance</li><li>Colorimetric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Online Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzers come in three main types: Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE), Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance, and Colorimetric. ISE analyzers measure ammonia concentration based on the electrical signal generated by ammonia ions. UV Absorbance analyzers use ultraviolet light to measure the amount of ammonia present in a sample. Colorimetric analyzers rely on color changes in a reaction between ammonia and a reagent to determine its concentration. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and ease of use.</p></p>
