<p><p>Monitoring systems for tunnel ventilation come in multipoint and detached market types. Multipoint systems involve multiple monitoring points throughout the tunnel to gather real-time data on air quality, temperature, and pressure. These systems provide a comprehensive view of the tunnel environment and allow for quick adjustments to ventilation systems. Detached market systems, on the other hand, are standalone units that are not integrated into the overall tunnel ventilation system. They provide localized monitoring in specific areas of the tunnel for targeted data collection and analysis.</p></p>
