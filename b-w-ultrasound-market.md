<p><p>B-W Ultrasound, also known as black and white ultrasound or greyscale ultrasound, is a widely used imaging technique in the medical field for visualizing internal organs and tissues. B-W Ultrasound is commonly used in obstetrics to monitor fetal development during pregnancy, as well as in diagnosing a variety of medical conditions.</p><p>The B-W Ultrasound Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period.
