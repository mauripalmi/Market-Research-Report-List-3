<p><strong>The Soft Membrane Potentiometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Membrane Potentiometer</li><li>Non-Contact Membrane Potentiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact Membrane Potentiometer consists of a resistive track and a wiper that makes physical contact with the track, allowing for precise and accurate measurement of displacement. On the other hand, Non-Contact Membrane Potentiometer uses non-contact sensing technology such as Hall sensors or inductive sensors to measure displacement without physical contact with the resistive track. This results in increased durability, longevity, and reliability, making it suitable for applications where physical wear and tear are a concern.</p></p>
