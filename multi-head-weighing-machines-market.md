<p><p>Multi-head weighing machines are commonly used in the packaging industry to accurately weigh and dispense products. There are two main types of multi-head weighing machines: Linear and Rotary. Linear machines feature a linear layout of weigh heads, each responsible for weighing a specific portion of the product. Rotary machines, on the other hand, have weigh heads arranged in a circular pattern, allowing for a higher throughput of products. Both types of machines offer high accuracy and speed in packaging processes.</p></p>
