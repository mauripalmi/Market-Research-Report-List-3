<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Lab Fume Cupboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Fume Cupboards</li><li>Auxiliary Air Fume Cupboards</li><li>Re-circulatory Fume Cupboards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab fume cupboards are used to protect laboratory personnel from harmful fumes and vapors. Conventional fume cupboards are the most common type, drawing air from the lab space and expelling it outside. Auxiliary air fume cupboards use additional air sources to improve containment efficiency. Re-circulatory fume cupboards filter and recirculate air back into the lab. Each type offers a different level of protection and ventilation control, catering to various laboratory requirements and preferences.</p></p>
