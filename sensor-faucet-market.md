<p><strong>The Sensor Faucet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infra-red Sensor Faucet</li><li>Touch Button Faucet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sensor faucets are available in two main types in the market: infra-red sensor faucets and touch button faucets. Infra-red sensor faucets use infrared technology to detect movement and automatically turn on the water flow when hands are placed under the faucet. Touch button faucets, on the other hand, require physical contact with a button to activate the water flow. Both types offer convenience and hygiene benefits by reducing the need to touch the faucet handles, thus minimizing the spread of germs.</p></p>
