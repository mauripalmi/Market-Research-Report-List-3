<p><p>Bioremediation technology and services encompass various methods to clean up contaminated environments. Phytoremediation involves using plants to absorb and break down pollutants. Biostimulation enhances natural microbial activity to degrade contaminants. Bioaugmentation introduces specific bacteria to enhance biodegradation. Bioreactors are controlled systems that support the growth of beneficial microorganisms. Fungal remediation uses fungi to break down contaminants. Land-based treatments involve techniques such as soil vapor extraction and bioventing to remove pollutants from the soil. Each method offers unique benefits for cleaning up contaminated sites.</p></p>
