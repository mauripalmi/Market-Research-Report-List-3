<p><p>Fully fluorinated fluoropolymers such as PTFE, FEP, and PFA are widely used in various industries due to their exceptional chemical resistance, high temperature stability, and low friction properties. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is known for its non-stick and non-reactive properties, making it ideal for cookware and industrial applications. Perfluoroalkoxy (PFA) is similar to PTFE but offers improved clarity and flexibility, suitable for tubing and lining applications. Fluorinated ethylene propylene (FEP) is a copolymer known for its high transparency and excellent electrical properties, commonly used in wire insulation and food processing equipment.</p></p>
