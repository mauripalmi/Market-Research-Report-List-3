<p><p>Metallurgy Additive Manufacturing for Aerospace involves the use of advanced technologies such as Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM) to produce complex metal parts with high precision and efficiency. SLM uses a high-power laser to selectively melt layers of metal powder, while EBM utilizes an electron beam to fuse metal powder together. These methods offer significant advantages in terms of design flexibility, reduced material waste, and faster production times, making them ideal for aerospace applications.</p></p>
