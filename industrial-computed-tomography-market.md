<p><strong>The Industrial Computed Tomography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Energy Industrial CT</li><li>Low Energy Industrial CT</li><li>Mini-Focus Industrial CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Computed Tomography (CT) is divided into three main types: High Energy Industrial CT, Low Energy Industrial CT, and Mini-Focus Industrial CT. High Energy Industrial CT uses higher X-ray energy levels for inspecting dense materials like metal components. Low Energy Industrial CT is used for inspecting lighter materials like plastics and composites. Mini-Focus Industrial CT is designed for smaller, high-resolution scans of delicate or intricate objects. Each type caters to different industrial needs and materials, providing a comprehensive solution for non-destructive testing and quality control.</p></p>
