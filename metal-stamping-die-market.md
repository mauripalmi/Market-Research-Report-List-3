<p><strong>The Metal Stamping Die Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Die</li><li>Transfer Die</li><li>Progressive Die</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal stamping die market is divided into three main types: Line Die, Transfer Die, and Progressive Die. Line Die is a single-stage die used for simple stamping processes. Transfer Die transfers the workpiece from one station to another, allowing for more complex stamping operations. Progressive Die is a multiple-stage die that performs several operations in a sequence as the workpiece moves through the die. Each type of die offers unique advantages and capabilities in the metal stamping industry.</p></p>
