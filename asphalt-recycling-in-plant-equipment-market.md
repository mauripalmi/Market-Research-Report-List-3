<p><p>Asphalt recycling in-plant equipment market consists of two main types: Hot Mix Regeneration and Cold Mix Regeneration. Hot Mix Regeneration involves heating and remixing existing asphalt materials to create new hot mix asphalt for use in road construction. Cold Mix Regeneration, on the other hand, involves using cold technology to rejuvenate and reuse old asphalt materials for patching and minor road repairs. Both types of equipment help in reducing waste and saving costs in the construction industry.</p></p>
