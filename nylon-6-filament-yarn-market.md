<p><p>Nylon 6 filament yarn is categorized into different market types based on their processing methods and properties. Flat yarn refers to a smooth and uniform surface texture ideal for weaving applications. POY (partially oriented yarn) is suitable for texturizing processes to enhance its elasticity and texture. FDY (fully drawn yarn) is manufactured with higher tensile strength and stability for use in knitting and weaving. HSO (high-shrinkage yarn) contracts significantly when exposed to heat, making it suitable for specific applications like heat setting or crepe yarn production.</p></p>
