<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IP Softswitch System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class 4 Softswitches</li><li>Class 5 Softswitches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Class 4 Softswitch is designed for use in wholesale VoIP services and connects different carriers' networks for routing large volumes of long-distance calls. It handles call routing and switching between carriers, optimizing call traffic for cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, a Class 5 Softswitch is used by telecom operators for providing services to end-users, such as residential or business customers. It manages local calling features like call waiting, call forwarding, and voicemail. Both types of softswitches play a crucial role in enabling efficient and reliable VoIP communication networks.</p></p>
