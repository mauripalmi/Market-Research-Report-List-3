<p><p>Submarine cables market can be classified into two main types: Impregnated Paper Cables and Self-Contained Oil-Filled Cables. Impregnated Paper Cables consist of layers of paper impregnated with oil to provide insulation, while Self-Contained Oil-Filled Cables have oil as both the insulation and coolant. These cables are crucial for transmitting data and power across long distances underwater, connecting continents and facilitating communication and commerce. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different underwater environments based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
