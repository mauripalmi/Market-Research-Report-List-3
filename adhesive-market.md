<p><strong>The Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive market is categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, easy to clean up, and have low VOC emissions. Solvent-based adhesives are known for their strong bonding properties and fast drying times. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature, melted when heated, and then solidify upon cooling. They are commonly used in packaging and woodworking applications. Each type offers unique characteristics and advantages catering to different industries and applications.</p></p>
