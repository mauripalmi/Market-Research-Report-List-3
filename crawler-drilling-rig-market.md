<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crawler drilling rigs come in two main types: pneumatic and hydraulic. Pneumatic crawler drilling rigs are powered by compressed air, making them suitable for environments where electricity or hydraulics are not readily available. On the other hand, hydraulic crawler drilling rigs utilize hydraulic fluid to power the rig's various mechanisms, offering greater control and efficiency in operation. Both types of rigs are commonly used in construction, mining, and geotechnical drilling applications.</p></p>
