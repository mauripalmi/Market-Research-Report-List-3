<p><p>Industrial abrasives market can be categorized into two main types - Metallic abrasives and Nonmetallic abrasives. Metallic abrasives, such as steel shot, are popular for applications requiring aggressive cutting and fast material removal. Nonmetallic abrasives, like silicon carbide and aluminum oxide, are known for their versatility and suitability for a wide range of materials. They are often used in finishing and polishing applications. Both types of abrasives play a crucial role in a variety of industries including automotive, construction, and metalworking.</p></p>
