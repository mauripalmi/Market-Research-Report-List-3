<p><ul><li>Rubber Mat-wire Net</li><li>Tire-Chain Net</li><li>Fiber Net</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic fenders have different market types based on their construction material. Rubber mat-wire net fenders are made of a combination of rubber matting with wire netting for added strength and durability. Tire-chain net fenders use old tires with chain netting for protection against impacts. Fiber net fenders are made from fibers woven into a net for maximum shock absorption. Other market types may include custom-made fenders with unique materials or designs. Each type offers varying levels of protection and performance in marine applications.</p></p>
