<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing Equipment (NDTE) market includes various types of equipment such as ultrasonic testing equipment, magnetic particle testing equipment, visual inspection equipment, radiography testing equipment, penetrant testing equipment, eddy current testing equipment, and acoustic emission testing equipment. Ultrasonic testing equipment uses high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws in materials. Magnetic particle testing equipment is used to identify surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Visual inspection equipment involves direct visual examination of components. Radiography testing equipment uses X-rays or gamma rays to detect internal defects. Penetrant testing equipment involves applying a liquid penetrant to surfaces to detect flaws. Eddy current testing equipment detects cracks and defects through electromagnetic induction. Acoustic emission testing equipment monitors the release of stress waves from materials to identify defects.</p></p>
