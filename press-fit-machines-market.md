<p><p>Press Fit Machines market can be classified into three main types: Manual Press Fit Machines, Semi-automatic Press Fit Machines, and Fully-automatic Press Fit Machines. </p><p>Manual Press Fit Machines require operators to manually load and unload workpieces and initiate the press fitting process. Semi-automatic Press Fit Machines involve some automated functions such as feeding and ejecting parts but still require operator intervention. </p><p>Fully-automatic Press Fit Machines are fully automated and can perform press fitting processes without any manual intervention, increasing productivity and efficiency.</p></p>
