<p><p>Cloud printing services can be categorized into three main markets: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Dual Cloud. Public Cloud printing services are managed and hosted by third-party service providers, offering efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Private Cloud printing services are internal cloud solutions managed within a company's own infrastructure, providing enhanced security and control. Dual Cloud printing services combine aspects of both public and private clouds, giving organizations the flexibility to utilize both internal and external resources for printing needs.</p></p>
