<p><strong>The Sheet Lamination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM)</li><li>Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sheet lamination market types include Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM) and Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM). UAM involves the ultrasonic welding of thin metal sheets, producing solid 3D parts. LOM involves layering and bonding of paper, plastic, or metal sheets, which are then cut and shaped using a laser or knife. Both methods are cost-effective and capable of producing complex geometries with high accuracy. They are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare for rapid prototyping and production of parts.</p></p>
