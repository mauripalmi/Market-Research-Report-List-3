<p><ul><li>Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li><li>Non-Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy floor coatings are categorized into two types: solvent-based and non-solvent-based. Solvent epoxy floor coatings contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and require adequate ventilation during application. Non-solvent epoxy floor coatings, on the other hand, are water-based and emit fewer harmful chemicals. Solvent-based coatings are known for their fast drying time and high durability, while non-solvent coatings are preferred for their environmentally friendly properties and low odor during application. Both types offer a protective and aesthetically pleasing finish for concrete floors.</p></p>
