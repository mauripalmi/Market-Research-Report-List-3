<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resonance Imaging</li><li>Computed Tomography</li><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Nuclear Imaging</li><li>X-ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peripheral angiography equipment market includes various types of imaging technologies like Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), Computed Tomography (CT), Ultrasound, Nuclear Imaging, and X-ray. MRI uses magnetic fields to create detailed images, while CT uses X-rays to produce cross-sectional images. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of blood vessels. Nuclear imaging involves injecting a radioactive substance into the bloodstream to create images. X-ray technology uses electromagnetic radiation to visualize blood vessels. These different types of equipment play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating peripheral artery disease.</p></p>
