<p><strong>The Residential Gas Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Gas Alarms</li><li>Portable Gas Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary gas alarms are installed in fixed locations within residential buildings to detect gas leaks and provide early warning to occupants. These alarms are typically hardwired into the building's infrastructure and are designed to continuously monitor for gas levels. </p><p>Portable gas alarms, on the other hand, are small, battery-operated devices that can be easily carried around and used in different locations within a residential setting. These alarms are ideal for individuals who may need to move around their home or travel with a gas detection device for added safety.</p></p>
