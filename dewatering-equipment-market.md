<p><p>Dewatering Equipment Market includes various types such as centrifuges, belt filter presses, vacuum filters, drying beds, sludge lagoons, and others. Centrifuges are used for high-efficiency solid-liquid separation. Belt filter presses are commonly used for dewatering sludge. Vacuum filters utilize vacuum pressure to separate solids from liquids. Drying beds involve the natural process of draining and drying sludge. Sludge lagoons store and treat sludge. Other types of dewatering equipment include screw presses, plate and frame filter presses, and filter bags which are used for specific applications.</p></p>
