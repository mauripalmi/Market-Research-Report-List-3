<p><p>Automated Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) come in two main types: Single Level Delivery and Dual Level Delivery. Single Level Delivery VLMs have a single picking bay where the goods are delivered and retrieved. On the other hand, Dual Level Delivery VLMs have two picking bays, allowing for simultaneous picking and putting away of goods. Both types of VLMs are used to maximize storage space, improve picking efficiency, and reduce labor costs in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
