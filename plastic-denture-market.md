<p><p>Plastic denture market includes both partial dentures and full dentures. Partial dentures are used when only some teeth are missing, and they are attached to the surrounding natural teeth with clasps or other connectors. Full dentures, on the other hand, are used when all teeth are missing and cover the entire upper or lower jaw. Both types of dentures are made from plastic materials such as acrylic resin, providing a cost-effective and durable solution for those needing tooth replacement.</p></p>
