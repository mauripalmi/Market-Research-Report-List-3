<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Transmission Fluids Market is divided into two major types: Manual Transmission Fluids and Automatic Transmission Fluids. Manual Transmission Fluids are specifically designed for vehicles with manual transmissions to provide smooth gear shifts and protect against wear and tear. Automatic Transmission Fluids, on the other hand, are formulated to work with automatic transmissions, ensuring proper lubrication, cooling, and hydraulic pressure control. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of commercial vehicle transmissions.</p></p>
