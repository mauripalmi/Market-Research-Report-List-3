<p><p>Ultraviolet generators are used for disinfection purposes in water and wastewater treatment. There are two main types in the market: Inline UV generators and Open Channel UV generators. Inline UV generators are installed directly into the water or wastewater pipeline for continuous disinfection. On the other hand, Open Channel UV generators are used in open channels or treatment tanks where water flows through. Both types use ultraviolet light to destroy microorganisms and pathogens, ensuring clean and safe water conditions.</p></p>
