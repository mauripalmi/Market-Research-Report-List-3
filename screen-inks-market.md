<p><p>Screen inks can be categorized into two main types: solvent-based printing inks and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based inks contain volatile organic compounds that evaporate during the drying process, resulting in a durable and fast-drying ink. On the other hand, water-based inks use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and safer for use. Both types of inks are widely used in the screen printing industry for a variety of applications, each offering unique benefits and characteristics.</p></p>
