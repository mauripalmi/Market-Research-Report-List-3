<p><p>Terahertz spectroscopy technology encompasses two main market types: Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy and Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy. Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy involves the measurement of the time it takes for terahertz radiation to propagate through a material, providing information on its properties. On the other hand, Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy analyzes the frequency components of terahertz radiation, offering insights into the molecular structure of materials. Both types of spectroscopy have applications in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, healthcare, and materials science.</p></p>
