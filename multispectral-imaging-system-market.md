<p><p>Multispectral Imaging System Market Types include single lens systems and multi-camera systems. Single lens systems utilize one lens to capture multiple spectral bands simultaneously, making them more compact and cost-effective. On the other hand, multi-camera systems use multiple cameras, each dedicated to a specific spectral band, allowing for higher resolution and accuracy in capturing different wavelengths. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications such as agriculture, environmental monitoring, and defense.</p></p>
