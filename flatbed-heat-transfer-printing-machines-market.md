<p><p>Flatbed heat transfer printing machines are available in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require more hands-on operation, with users needing to manually adjust settings and feed materials. Semi-automatic machines offer some automation, such as automatic feeding and cooling, but still require some manual intervention. Fully automatic machines are the most advanced, with features like automatic temperature control, pressure adjustment, and material handling, requiring minimal human intervention. These different types cater to varying levels of user expertise and production needs in the market.</p></p>
