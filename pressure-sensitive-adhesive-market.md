<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Hot Melt & Radiation</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure-sensitive adhesives are used in various industries and can be classified into different types based on the composition of the adhesive. The four main categories are water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, and radiation-curable adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low VOC emissions. Solvent-based adhesives offer good adhesion to a wide range of substrates. Hot melt adhesives provide fast setting and high initial bond strength. Radiation-curable adhesives are cured through exposure to UV or electron beam radiation. Other types of pressure-sensitive adhesives may include acrylic adhesives, rubber-based adhesives, and silicone adhesives.</p></p>
