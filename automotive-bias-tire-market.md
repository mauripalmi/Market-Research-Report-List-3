<p><p>An automotive bias tire is a type of tire construction in which the internal ply cords are angled at 30-40 degrees with respect to the centerline of the tire. This design provides strength and stability for heavy-duty vehicles such as trucks, buses, and agricultural machinery. Automotive bias tires are known for their durability and resistance to damage from rough terrains.</p><p>The Automotive Bias Tire Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
