<p><p>RF shielded rooms are essential for blocking electromagnetic interference. There are three types of market categories for these rooms based on their construction method: Manual, Pneumatic, and Electric. Manual RF shielded rooms require physical effort to open and close the door. Pneumatic rooms use compressed air to operate the door, offering ease of use. Electric rooms have automated door systems controlled by electricity for seamless access. Each type offers varying levels of convenience and functionality to meet the specific needs of users.</p></p>
