<p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) resins are widely used in various industries including construction, automotive, and packaging. The PVC market is categorized into three main types based on production methods: suspension, emulsion, and blended. Suspension PVC is produced by a polymerization process in a water-based medium, while emulsion PVC is created through a process that involves the dispersion of PVC particles in water. Blended PVC combines suspension and emulsion PVC to enhance specific properties for different applications in the market. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits for various end-uses.</p></p>
