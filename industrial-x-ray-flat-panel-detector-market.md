<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial X-Ray Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-Ray Flat Panel Detectors are categorized into two types: Direct Conversion and Indirect Conversion. </p><p>Direct Conversion detectors directly convert X-ray photons into electrical signals using materials like selenium or cadmium telluride. This leads to high image quality and efficiency but can come at a higher cost.</p><p>Indirect Conversion detectors convert X-ray photons into light, which is then converted into electrical signals using a scintillator material like cesium iodide. This method is typically more cost-effective but may result in slightly lower image quality compared to Direct Conversion detectors.</p></p>
