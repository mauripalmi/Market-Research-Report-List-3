<p><ul><li>Oil-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood coatings come in three main types: oil-based, water-based, and solvent-based. Oil-based coatings are known for their durability and resistance to wear and tear, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up, making them a popular choice for indoor projects. Solvent-based coatings offer a strong and durable finish, but they emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that can be harmful to the environment and human health. Each type of wood coating caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
