<p><p>A solar powered ATM is an automated teller machine that is powered by solar energy instead of traditional electricity sources. These ATMs are equipped with solar panels that capture sunlight and convert it into electricity to operate the machine. This eco-friendly solution helps reduce energy costs and carbon emissions, making it a sustainable option for remote or off-grid locations where electricity supply is unreliable or unavailable.</p><p>The Solar Powered ATM Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period. Governments and financial institutions are also incentivizing the adoption of solar powered ATMs through various policies and initiatives.</p><p>The latest trends in the Solar Powered ATM Market include advancements in solar panel technology, integration of smart features such as biometric authentication and contactless payments, and the development of portable and mobile ATMs powered by solar energy.
