<p><p>Spring brake chambers are crucial components in air brake systems of commercial vehicles. There are two main types of spring brake chambers in the market: diaphragm type and piston type. Diaphragm type chambers use a flexible rubber diaphragm to actuate the brakes, while piston type chambers use a metal piston. Both types operate on the principle of air pressure to engage brake mechanisms when the vehicle is parked or during emergency situations. These chambers are available in various sizes and configurations to meet the specific needs of different vehicles.</p></p>
