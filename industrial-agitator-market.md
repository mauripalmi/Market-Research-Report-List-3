The market is segmented into three main types: top-entry agitators, which are suitable for large tanks with high viscosity fluids; side-entry agitators, ideal for mixing in cylindrical tanks; and bottom-entry agitators, which are commonly used for blending in smaller tanks. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements of the industry.</p></p>
