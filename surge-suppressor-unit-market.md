<p><strong>The Surge Suppressor Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Flow Or Choke Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surge suppressor units are available in different types such as Switch Type, Pressure Limiting Type, and Flow or Choke Type. Switch Type surge suppressors operate by diverting excess voltage to ground when a surge occurs. Pressure Limiting Type units limit the voltage level to a safe threshold during a surge. Flow or Choke Type suppressors restrict the flow of excess current during a surge. Each type offers unique features and benefits for protecting electrical equipment from power surges.</p></p>
