<p><p>Medical gas fittings market is divided into various types including connectors, pipe plugs, adapters, couplers, and hoses. Connectors are used to connect different medical gas equipment securely. Pipe plugs are used to seal off unused gas lines. Adapters are used to connect incompatible fittings. Couplers allow for quick and easy connection and disconnection of gas lines. Hoses are flexible tubes that carry gases between equipment. Each type of fitting serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safe and efficient delivery of medical gases.</p></p>
