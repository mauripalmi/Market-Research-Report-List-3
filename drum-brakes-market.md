<p><ul><li>Leading Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Two Leading Shoe Brake</li><li>Servo Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum brakes market types include Leading Trailing Shoe Brake, Two Leading Shoe Brake, and Servo Brake. Leading Trailing Shoe Brake features one leading and one trailing shoe that press outward against the drum. Two Leading Shoe Brake has two leading shoes that press outward against the drum. Servo Brake uses hydraulic pressure to amplify the pressure applied to the shoes. These types of drum brakes offer different mechanisms for friction and stopping power, catering to various needs and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
