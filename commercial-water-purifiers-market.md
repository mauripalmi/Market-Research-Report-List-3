<p><p>Commercial water purifiers are available in various types, including activated carbon, UV technology, reverse osmosis, and others. Activated carbon purifiers remove impurities through a physical process of absorption. UV technology uses ultraviolet light to disinfect water by killing harmful microorganisms. Reverse osmosis purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to filter out contaminants. Other types of purifiers may include ion exchange or distillation methods. Each type of purifier offers unique advantages and is suited to different water purification needs in commercial settings.</p></p>
