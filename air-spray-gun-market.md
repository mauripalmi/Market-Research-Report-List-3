<p><ul><li>Straight Nozzle</li><li>Angled Nozzle</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air spray guns are classified into different types based on the design of the nozzle. Straight nozzle air spray guns have a nozzle that is aligned straight with the gun body, providing a direct and precise spraying pattern. Angled nozzle air spray guns have a nozzle that is angled to provide better reach and coverage in hard-to-reach areas. Other types of air spray guns may include adjustable nozzles, HVLP (high volume low pressure) guns, and mini spray guns, each offering unique features and benefits for specific painting applications.</p></p>
