<p><p>The Open Angle Glaucoma Drugs Market is segmented into four main types based on the mechanism of action: Prostaglandins, Carbonic Anhydrase Inhibitors, Cholinergic Drugs, and Others. Prostaglandins work by increasing the outflow of aqueous humor from the eye. Carbonic Anhydrase Inhibitors reduce the production of aqueous humor. Cholinergic Drugs increase the drainage of aqueous humor from the eye. The "Others" category includes miscellaneous drugs with different mechanisms of action for treating open-angle glaucoma. These drugs play a crucial role in managing the condition and preserving vision.</p></p>
