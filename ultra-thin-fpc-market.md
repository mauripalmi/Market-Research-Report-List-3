<p><ul><li>Single-sided</li><li>Double-sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultra thin FPC (Flexible Printed Circuit) market consists of two main types: Single-sided and Double-sided. Single-sided FPCs have circuitry on only one side of the flexible substrate, making them more cost-effective and suitable for simpler applications. On the other hand, Double-sided FPCs have circuitry on both sides of the substrate, allowing for more complex designs and greater functionality. Both types offer flexibility, lightweight construction, and high reliability, making them ideal for use in various electronic devices and applications.</p></p>
