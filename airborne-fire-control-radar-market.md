<p><p>Airborne Fire Control Radar market is categorized based on the frequency bands it operates in. S-band radars operate in the 2-4 GHz frequency range, offering long-range detection and tracking capabilities. X-band radars operate in the 8-12 GHz frequency range, providing higher resolution and accuracy for targeting. Ku/K/Ka band radars operate in the 12-40 GHz frequency range, offering even greater resolution and sensitivity for precise targeting and tracking. Each type of radar serves specific purposes in airborne fire control systems, catering to different operational requirements.</p></p>
