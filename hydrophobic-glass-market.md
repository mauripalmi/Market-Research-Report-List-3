<p><p>Hydrophobic Glass market includes two main types: Hydrophobic Coating Technology and Nanofilm Technology. Hydrophobic Coating Technology involves applying a thin layer of hydrophobic material on the glass surface, which helps repel water and prevent streaks. Nanofilm Technology, on the other hand, uses nanotechnology to create an ultra-thin film on the glass surface that repels water and dirt more effectively. Both technologies aim to make glass surfaces water-resistant, easier to clean, and improve visibility during rainy conditions.</p></p>
