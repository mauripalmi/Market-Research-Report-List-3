<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol Copolymer (EVOH) is categorized into different market types based on the ethylene content present in the polymer. EVOH with ethylene content below 29 mol% is suitable for high gas barrier applications. EVOH with ethylene content between 29-35 mol% is used for food packaging. EVOH with ethylene content between 35-38 mol% is ideal for packaging liquids. EVOH with ethylene content between 38-44 mol% is commonly used for packaging industrial chemicals. EVOH with ethylene content above 44 mol% is preferred for extrusion blow molding applications.</p></p>
