<p><p>Cold forming press market consists of two main types: electric and hydraulic presses. Electric presses are powered by electricity and are ideal for small to medium sized operations due to their lower energy consumption and quieter operation. On the other hand, hydraulic presses are powered by hydraulic systems and are more suitable for high force applications where precision and control are crucial. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
