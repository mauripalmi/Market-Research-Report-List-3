<p><strong>The Smart Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Meter Reading (AMR)</li><li>Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) is a type of smart gas meter system that allows for the remote collection of gas usage data. AMR systems use communication technology to automatically transmit gas consumption data to utility companies, eliminating the need for manual readings. </p><p>Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) is a more advanced form of smart gas metering that not only enables remote data collection but also facilitates two-way communication between the meter and utility company. AMI systems provide real-time data on gas consumption, improve operational efficiency, and enable customers to better manage their energy usage.</p></p>
