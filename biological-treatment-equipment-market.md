<p><p>Biological treatment equipment market includes dry fermentation and wet fermentation technologies. Dry fermentation involves organic waste decomposition in the absence of water, producing biogas and compost. It is suitable for solid waste like agricultural residues. Wet fermentation, on the other hand, uses water in the decomposition process, producing biogas, nutrient-rich digestate, and sludge. This method is ideal for liquid waste such as sewage and food waste. Both technologies help in waste management, energy production, and nutrient recycling.</p></p>
