<p><p>Hydrolyzed Milk Protein market is divided into two main types: Whey and Casein. Whey protein is derived from the liquid part of milk and is quickly absorbed by the body, making it popular among athletes and fitness enthusiasts. On the other hand, Casein protein is derived from the solid part of milk and is absorbed slowly, providing a sustained release of amino acids. Both Whey and Casein proteins are widely used in sports nutrition products, supplements, and functional foods due to their numerous health benefits.</p></p>
