<p><p>Forklift jacks are commonly available in two main types: Electric and Hydraulic. Electric forklift jacks are powered by electricity and are typically used for lighter loads and indoor applications. They are easy to operate and require minimal maintenance. On the other hand, Hydraulic forklift jacks are powered by hydraulic fluid and are more versatile, capable of handling heavier loads and rougher terrains. They are durable and offer smooth and reliable lifting operations. Both types of forklift jacks have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
