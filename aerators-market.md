<p><p>Fixed Aerators are stationary devices that are installed at the bottom of water bodies to help increase oxygen levels in the water. They are commonly used in ponds, lakes, and wastewater treatment systems. Floating Aerators, on the other hand, are devices that float on the surface of the water and help to aerate the water. They are often used in decorative water features, golf course ponds, and aquaculture systems. Both types of aerators help to improve water quality and support aquatic life.</p></p>
