<p><p>The motorcycles clutch market can be divided into two main types: manual and semi-automatic. Manual clutches require the rider to manually engage and disengage the clutch by pulling a lever, providing complete control over gear shifting. On the other hand, semi-automatic clutches use a combination of manual and automatic technology, allowing the rider to shift gears without using a clutch lever. Both types have their own benefits and cater to different preferences of motorcycle riders in terms of convenience and control.</p></p>
