<p><strong>The Semiconductor Packaging Mold Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transfer Molds</li><li>Compression Molds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor packaging mold market is divided into two main types - Transfer Molds and Compression Molds. Transfer Molds are used to transfer the molten material into the mold cavity in a single step, ensuring precise filling and reducing air entrapment. Compression Molds, on the other hand, use a two-step process where the material is first preformed before being transferred into the final mold cavity for compression. Both types of molds play a vital role in the semiconductor packaging industry by facilitating the production of high-quality packaging components.</p></p>
