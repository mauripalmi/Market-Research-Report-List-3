Threaded connections are convenient for easy installation and removal. Flanged connections provide a secure and leak-proof connection for high-pressure applications. Butt weld connections offer a strong and permanent connection for industrial applications. Socket weld connections provide a reliable and compact connection for small pipe sizes. Each type of connection offers unique advantages and is suitable for different operating conditions and industry requirements.</p></p>
