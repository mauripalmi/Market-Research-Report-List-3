<p><p>OLED cellphone display market can be categorized into two main types: PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED). PMOLED technology is simpler and more cost-effective, making it suitable for small displays like those on feature phones. On the other hand, AMOLED technology offers higher resolution, faster refresh rates, and better energy efficiency, making it more suitable for high-end smartphones. Both PMOLED and AMOLED have their own unique advantages and applications in the fast-growing smartphone industry.</p></p>
