<p><p>Digital Marine Communication Software has two main market types: Internal Communication and External Communication. Internal Communication software is used for smooth communication and collaboration among crew members and staff within the vessel. It includes features such as messaging, file sharing, task management, and scheduling. External Communication software, on the other hand, enables communication with external parties such as port authorities, clients, and suppliers. It focuses on ensuring efficient and secure communication while the vessel is at sea.</p></p>
