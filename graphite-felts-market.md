<p><p>Graphite felts are a type of material used in various industrial applications, such as batteries and fuel cells. There are three main types of graphite felts based on the raw material used to produce them: Rayon-based, PAN-based, and others. Rayon-based graphite felts are made from cellulose fibers, offering good flexibility and thermal stability. PAN-based graphite felts are produced from polyacrylonitrile fibers, providing high strength and durability. The category of "others" includes graphite felts made from alternative materials with varying properties and uses in the market.</p></p>
