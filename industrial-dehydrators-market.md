<p><strong>The Industrial Dehydrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conduction Type</li><li>Convection Type</li><li>Radiation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial dehydrators are classified into three main types based on the method of heat transfer used - conduction type, convection type, and radiation type. Conduction type dehydrators transfer heat through direct contact with the material being dried, convection type dehydrators circulate hot air to remove moisture, and radiation type dehydrators use electromagnetic waves for heating. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on factors such as the type of material being dried and the desired end product.</p></p>
