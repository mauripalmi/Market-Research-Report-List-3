<p><p>Metal noise barriers are commonly categorized into two types: ground-mounted noise barriers and structure-mounted noise barriers. Ground-mounted noise barriers are installed on the ground level to block noise coming from roads, highways, or industrial areas, while structure-mounted noise barriers are attached to existing structures such as bridges, buildings, or walls to reduce noise transmission. Both types of barriers are made of metal materials like steel or aluminum to provide effective sound insulation and improve the acoustic environment in urban areas.</p></p>
