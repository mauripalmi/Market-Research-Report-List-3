<p><p>Air duct heaters come in two main types: low temperature and high temperature. Low temperature air duct heaters are typically used in applications where the required air temperature is below 750°F. They are commonly used in industrial processes such as drying and curing. On the other hand, high temperature air duct heaters are suitable for applications where temperatures above 750°F are required, such as in industrial furnaces and kilns. Both types of heaters play a crucial role in maintaining desired air temperatures in various industrial processes.</p></p>
