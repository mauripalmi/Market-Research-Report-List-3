Passive Drool Collection involves allowing saliva to naturally accumulate in the mouth and then spitting it into a designated container for analysis. Oral Swab Collection, on the other hand, requires using a swab to collect saliva from the inside of the mouth. Both methods offer non-invasive ways to collect saliva samples for cancer testing, providing convenient and efficient options for early detection and monitoring.</p></p>
