Spunlace technology involves high-pressure water jets to create a strong and absorbent material. Airlaid technology uses air to create a fluffy and soft material. Wetlaid technology utilizes a combination of water and chemicals to form a smooth and durable material. Spunlaid technology involves spinning fibers to create a nonwoven fabric. Each technology offers unique properties and benefits for various wet tissue and wipe applications.</p></p>
