<p><p>Vertical conveyors are machines used to move materials and products vertically from one level to another. There are various types of vertical conveyors which include continuous vertical conveyors, vertical reciprocating lifts, circulating conveyors, vertical baggage lifts, and other specialized models. Continuous vertical conveyors feature a continuous loop for transporting items, while vertical reciprocating lifts move products in a straight up and down motion. Circulating conveyors move products in a circular motion, and vertical baggage lifts are commonly used in airports. Other specialized vertical conveyors cater to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
