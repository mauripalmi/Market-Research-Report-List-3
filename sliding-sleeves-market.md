<p><strong>The Sliding Sleeves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open/Close</li><li>Choking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sliding sleeves in the oil and gas industry come in different types based on their functionality. Open/close sliding sleeves are used to selectively control the flow of hydrocarbons in the wellbore by opening or closing the sleeve. On the other hand, choking sliding sleeves have the ability to regulate the flow rate by adjusting the size of the choke orifice. Both types of sliding sleeves play a crucial role in optimizing production and managing reservoir pressure in oil and gas wells.</p></p>
