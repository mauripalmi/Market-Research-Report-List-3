<p><p>Edge banders are machines used to apply edging materials to the edges of wood-based panels, providing a finished look. There are two main types of edge banders in the market: single-sided edge banders and double-sided edge banders. Single-sided edge banders apply edging material to one side of the panel at a time, while double-sided edge banders can apply edging material to both sides simultaneously. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on the requirements of the woodworking project.</p></p>
