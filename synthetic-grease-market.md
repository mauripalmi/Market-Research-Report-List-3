<p><p>Synthetic grease can be classified into different types based on their composition: inorganic, organic, hydrocarbon, and other markets. Inorganic synthetic greases are made from materials like silica and are known for their high thermal stability. Organic synthetic greases are composed of materials like polyurea and are preferred for their compatibility with most plastics and rubbers. Hydrocarbon synthetic greases are made from materials like PAO and offer excellent resistance to oxidation. Other markets include specialty synthetic greases tailored for specific applications like extreme temperatures or high speeds.</p></p>
