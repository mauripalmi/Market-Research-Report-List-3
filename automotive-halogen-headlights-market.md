<p><p>Automotive halogen headlights market consists of two main types based on their function: low beam lights and high beam lights. Low beam lights are designed to illuminate the road ahead without blinding oncoming traffic, providing a wider but shorter range of light. High beam lights, on the other hand, are used to provide maximum visibility for the driver in dark or poorly lit conditions, illuminating a longer and narrower range ahead. Both types serve different purposes and enhance driving safety in various situations.</p></p>
