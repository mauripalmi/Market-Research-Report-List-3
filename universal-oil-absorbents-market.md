<p><p>Universal oil absorbents are divided into three main types based on their composition: Natural Organic, Natural Inorganic, and Synthetic Material markets. Natural Organic absorbents are derived from plants, such as peat moss and coconut husks, and are biodegradable. Natural Inorganic absorbents, such as clay and volcanic rock, are non-biodegradable but highly effective at absorbing oil. Synthetic Material absorbents, like polypropylene and polyethylene, are man-made and also effective at absorbing oil. Each type of absorbent has its own unique properties and benefits for different situations.</p></p>
