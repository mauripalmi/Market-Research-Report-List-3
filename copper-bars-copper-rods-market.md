<p><p>Copper bars, also known as copper rods, come in different types based on their composition and purity. Some common market types include EC (Electrolytic Tough Pitch), ETP (Electrolytic Tough Pitch), DHP (Deoxidized High Phosphorus), Oxygen Free, Silver Bearing, and Others. EC copper bars are known for their high electrical conductivity, ETP copper bars for their excellent formability, DHP copper bars for their high thermal conductivity, and Oxygen Free copper bars for their low oxygen content. Silver Bearing copper bars contain added silver for improved strength and conductivity.</p></p>
