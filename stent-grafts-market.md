<p><ul><li>AAA Stent Grafts</li><li>TAA Stent Grafts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AAA Stent Grafts are specifically designed for patients with abdominal aortic aneurysms, a condition where the lower part of the aorta becomes enlarged. These stent grafts help to reinforce and strengthen the weakened area of the aorta, preventing potentially life-threatening ruptures. On the other hand, TAA Stent Grafts are used for patients with thoracic aortic aneurysms, which occur in the chest area. These stent grafts provide a minimally invasive alternative to traditional open surgery, reducing risks and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
