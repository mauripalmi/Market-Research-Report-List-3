<p><p>Ultrafiltration System market consists of two main types: Polymeric and Ceramic. Polymeric membranes are made of synthetic materials such as polysulfone or polyethersulfone, offering high fouling resistance and flexibility in design. On the other hand, Ceramic membranes are composed of inorganic materials like alumina or zirconia, providing high chemical resistance and durability. Both types are widely used in water treatment, pharmaceutical, and food & beverage industries for their efficient removal of particles and solutes, contributing to clean and safe water purification processes.</p></p>
