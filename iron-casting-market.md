<p><ul><li>Gray iron</li><li>Ductile iron</li><li>Malleable iron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron casting market is typically classified into three main types: gray iron, ductile iron, and malleable iron. Gray iron has excellent machinability and good thermal conductivity, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. Ductile iron is known for its high tensile strength, toughness, and impact resistance, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Malleable iron combines the properties of both gray and ductile iron, offering good ductility and strength. Each type caters to different market demands and applications within the iron casting industry.</p></p>
