<p><p>Speech Recognition Software market types include Isolated Word Recognition, Keyword Spotting, and Continuous Speech Recognition. Isolated Word Recognition involves recognizing individual words spoken by a user. Keyword Spotting recognizes specific words or phrases in a larger body of speech. Continuous Speech Recognition allows for the continuous interpretation of spoken language without breaks between words. These market types cater to different needs and use cases, such as voice commands, transcription, and dictation, offering a range of solutions for various industries and applications.</p></p>
