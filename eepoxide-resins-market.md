<p><p>Epoxy resins can be categorized into different types based on their physical form and chemical composition. Liquid epoxy resins are typically used for coatings and adhesives, while solid epoxy resins are more commonly used in composites and electrical applications. Halogenated epoxy resins contain halogen atoms, providing flame retardant properties. Multifunctional epoxy resins have additional functional groups for enhanced performance. Other epoxy resins include specialty formulations tailored for specific applications such as sealants or potting compounds. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for various industries.</p></p>
