<p><p>Investment casting parts market types refer to different processes used in the manufacturing of investment cast parts. The Sodium Silicate process involves creating molds using a mixture of sodium silicate and silica sand. On the other hand, the Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/Silica Sol process involves the use of a sol-gel technique to create ceramic shells for casting. Both processes offer unique advantages in terms of precision, surface finish, and production versatility in the investment casting industry.</p></p>
