<p><p>Digital refractometers are classified into three main types based on their design and usage. </p><p>1. Digital handheld refractometers are portable, easy-to-use devices commonly used in the field or on-the-go. They are ideal for quick and accurate measurements of samples.</p><p>2. They offer higher accuracy and precision, making them suitable for research and quality control applications.</p><p>3. They are crucial for industries requiring constant monitoring of product parameters.</p></p>
