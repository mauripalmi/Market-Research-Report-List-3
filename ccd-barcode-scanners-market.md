<p><p>CCD barcode scanners are available in two main types - wired and wireless. Wired CCD barcode scanners require a physical connection to a computer or other device through a cable for data transmission. On the other hand, wireless CCD barcode scanners use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit data wirelessly to a connected device. Both types offer reliable and efficient scanning capabilities, with wired scanners being suitable for fixed scanning stations and wireless scanners providing flexibility and mobility for on-the-go scanning tasks.</p></p>
