Pressure transducers are used for measuring fluid pressure in pipelines and tanks. Other types of transducers can include strain gauges for measuring stress in materials, and accelerometers for monitoring vibrations in structures. Each type of transducer plays a vital role in the civil engineering industry.</p></p>
