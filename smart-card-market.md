<p><strong>The Smart Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Cards</li><li>Contactless Cards</li><li>Memory Cards</li><li>CPU/MPU Microprocessor Multifunction Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart cards can be classified into four main types: contact cards, contactless cards, memory cards, and CPU/MPU microprocessor multifunction cards. Contact cards require physical contact with a card reader for data transfer, while contactless cards use radio-frequency technology for communication. Memory cards store data but do not have processing capabilities, while CPU/MPU microprocessor multifunction cards have a microprocessor for advanced security and multiple functions. Each type caters to different needs and security levels in various industries such as finance, healthcare, and transportation.</p></p>
