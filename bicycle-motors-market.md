<p><p>Hub motor and crank motor are two main types of bicycle motors in the market. Hub motor is directly integrated into the wheel hub, providing an easy and cost-effective option for electric bike conversion kits. On the other hand, crank motors are mounted near the bike's pedals, offering pedal assist and a more natural riding experience. Hub motors are popular for their simplicity and affordability, while crank motors are preferred for their efficiency and performance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the bicycle market.</p></p>
