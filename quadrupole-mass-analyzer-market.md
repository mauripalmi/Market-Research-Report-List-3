<p><p>Quadrupole Mass Analyzers have two main types: Single Quadrupole Mass Analyzer and Triple Quadrupole Mass Analyzer. </p><p>Single Quadrupole Mass Analyzers are used for qualitative analysis, identifying the mass-to-charge ratio of ions in a sample. They are commonly used in research labs for studying molecular structure and composition.</p><p>Triple Quadrupole Mass Analyzers are used for quantitative analysis, measuring the abundance of specific ions in a sample. They are popular in industries like pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety for their high sensitivity and accuracy.</p></p>
