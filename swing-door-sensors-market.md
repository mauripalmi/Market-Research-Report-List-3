<p><p>Swing door sensors market types can be classified based on product type and technology. Product types include surface contact sensors, recessed contact sensors, vanishing sensors, and vented sensors. Surface contact sensors are mounted on the surface of the door, while recessed sensors are embedded within the door frame. Vanishing sensors are hidden within the door structure, and vented sensors allow for air flow. Technology options include active infrared, auto focus position sensitive detection (PSD), microwave, and others. Each type offers unique benefits for automated swing door systems.</p></p>
