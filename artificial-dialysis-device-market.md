<p><p>The Artificial Dialysis Device Market consists of two main types: Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis. </p><p>Hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter waste and excess fluids from the blood, typically done in a clinic setting. Peritoneal Dialysis, on the other hand, involves using the lining of the abdomen to filter waste and excess fluids from the blood, typically done at home. Both types of dialysis are crucial for patients with kidney failure to maintain proper fluid and waste balance in the body.</p></p>
