<p><p>Rail welding machines are categorized into mobile machines and stationary machines in the market. Mobile machines are designed to be easily transportable and can be used for on-site welding operations. They are often preferred for maintenance and repair work on railways. On the other hand, stationary machines are permanently installed at a fixed location and are best suited for high-volume production welding in manufacturing plants. Both types of machines offer efficient and precise welding solutions for rail applications.</p></p>
