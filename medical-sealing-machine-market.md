<p><p>Medical sealing machines are commonly classified into two main types based on their functionality: Continuous Non-print Sealer and Continuous Print Sealer. Continuous Non-print Sealers are designed to seal medical packages without any printing capability, providing a simple and efficient sealing solution. On the other hand, Continuous Print Sealers are equipped with printing functionality, allowing for the customization of packages with vital information such as expiration dates and lot numbers. Both types play a crucial role in the medical packaging industry, catering to different sealing needs.</p></p>
