<p><ul><li>anchor and buckle pretensioners</li><li>seat belt retractor pretensioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive seat belt pretensioners are devices that retract and tighten the seat belt around the occupant during a crash to reduce forward movement. Two types are anchor and buckle pretensioners, which work by either tightening the belt where it attaches to the vehicle (anchor) or at the buckle where it secures the occupant (buckle). Seat belt retractor pretensioners use a pyrotechnic device to retract the seat belt rapidly in the event of a crash, further restraining the occupant.</p></p>
