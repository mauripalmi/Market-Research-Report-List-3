<p><p>The commercial automatic coffee machine market can be divided into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic machines are equipped with advanced technology that handles the entire coffee-making process from grinding the beans to delivering the final product, requiring minimal human intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines give the user more control over the brewing process, allowing them to adjust settings like water temperature and shot length.
