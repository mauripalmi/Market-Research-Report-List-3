<p><ul><li>Photocatalytic Oxidation</li><li>Bi-polar Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ionic air purifiers utilize different technologies to clean the air within a space. Photocatalytic oxidation works by converting pollutants into harmless compounds through a reaction with UV light and a catalyst. Bi-polar ionization releases positively and negatively charged ions into the air to neutralize pollutants. Both types of purifiers help to improve indoor air quality by eliminating harmful particles and odors. Consumers can choose the technology that best suits their needs based on the size of the space and the specific pollutants present.</p></p>
