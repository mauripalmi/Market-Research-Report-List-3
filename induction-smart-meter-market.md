<p><p>Induction smart meters are widely used in the energy sector and can be categorized into two main types: Direct Current (DC) meters and Alternating Current (AC) meters. DC meters measure and monitor the flow of electricity in a single direction, often used in systems with solar panels or batteries. AC meters, on the other hand, measure electricity flowing back and forth in a circuit, commonly found in residential and commercial buildings. Both types of meters play a crucial role in monitoring energy consumption and promoting energy efficiency.</p></p>
