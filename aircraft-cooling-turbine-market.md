<p><p>Aircraft Cooling Turbine market includes two main types: Gas Cooling Turbine and Water Cooling Turbine. Gas Cooling Turbines use compressed air or other gases to cool the aircraft engine, while Water Cooling Turbines use liquid coolant, typically water, to regulate the temperature of the engine. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining safe operating temperatures for aircraft engines, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. The choice between Gas and Water Cooling Turbines depends on factors such as engine size, operating conditions, and cost considerations.</p></p>
