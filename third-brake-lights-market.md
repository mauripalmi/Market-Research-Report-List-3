<p><p>Gas brake lights are traditional brake lights that use a gas bulb to produce light when the brake pedal is pressed. On the other hand, LED brake lights use light-emitting diodes to illuminate when the brake is applied. LED brake lights are known for their brighter illumination and longer lifespan compared to gas brake lights. Both types of brake lights are essential safety features in vehicles, helping to alert following drivers of braking actions to prevent rear-end collisions.</p></p>
