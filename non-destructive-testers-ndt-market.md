<p><p>Non Destructive Testers (NDT) market includes different types of testing methods such as Magnetic Particle Tester, Ultrasonic Tester, Liquid Penetrant Tester, and others. Magnetic Particle Tester is used to detect surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials. Ultrasonic Tester uses sound waves to detect internal defects in materials. Liquid Penetrant Tester involves applying a liquid dye to surface defects. Other methods may include Radiographic Testing, Eddy Current Testing, and Visual Testing, all aimed at inspecting materials without causing damage.</p></p>
