<p><ul><li>Curved Disc Washer</li><li>Wave Washer</li><li>Split Washer</li><li>Toothed Lock Washer</li><li>Tab Washer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Locking washers are classified into different types based on their design and application. Curved disc washers provide a secure fastening by exerting pressure on the nut or bolt. Wave washers have a wave-like shape to absorb shock and prevent loosening. Split washers have a split design to create tension and prevent rotation. Toothed lock washers have teeth that bite into the surface for a secure grip. Tab washers have tabs that fold over the nut or bolt to prevent loosening. Each type is used in specific applications to ensure a tight and secure fastening.</p></p>
