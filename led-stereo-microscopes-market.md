<p><p>LED Stereo Microscopes come in three main types - Monocular, Binocular, and Trinocular. Monocular microscopes have one eyepiece for viewing, making them more compact and affordable, suitable for basic applications. Binocular microscopes have two eyepieces for stereoscopic viewing, providing a more immersive experience and better depth perception. Trinocular microscopes have an additional third eyepiece for attaching a camera or other imaging device, allowing for documentation and sharing of images. Each type offers different features and benefits depending on the user's needs.</p></p>
