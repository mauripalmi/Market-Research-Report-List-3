<p><p>Power supply isolation equipment can be classified into different types based on the insulation media used for isolation. Insulation media power supply isolation equipment isolates the input and output using materials such as silicon dioxide or plastic. Air gap power supply isolation equipment creates isolation by physically separating the input and output circuits with an air gap. Other types of isolation equipment may utilize different methods for achieving isolation, such as magnetic or optical isolation techniques. Each type of power supply isolation equipment has its own strengths and applications in various industries.</p></p>
