<p><ul><li>Fluid Separation Alarm</li><li>Fluid Immersion Alarm</li><li>Pressure Change Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluid alarm instruments are used in various industries to monitor fluid levels and pressure changes. Fluid separation alarms are used to detect when two different fluids are mixing unintentionally. Fluid immersion alarms are used to alert users when a device or equipment is immersed in a fluid. Pressure change alarms monitor fluctuations in pressure levels within a system. These alarm instruments help prevent potential damage or accidents by providing early warnings to operators.</p></p>
