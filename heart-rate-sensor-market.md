<p><p>Heart Rate Sensor market consists of two main types - Photoplethysmography (PPG) Sensors and Electrocardiography (ECG) Sensors. PPG Sensors measure heart rate by shining light into the skin and detecting blood volume changes, while ECG Sensors measure heart rate by detecting the electrical activity of the heart. Both types of sensors are used in various wearable devices and medical equipment to monitor heart rate and provide valuable health data to users. The market for these sensors continues to grow as the demand for advanced health monitoring technology increases.</p></p>
