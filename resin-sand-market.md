<p><p>Resin sand market is segmented into two main types: Resin Coated Sand and Resin Bonded Sand. Resin Coated Sand is a type of silica sand coated with phenolic resin to improve mold strength and reduce casting defects. On the other hand, Resin Bonded Sand is a mixture of resin and sand, used for creating molds and cores in foundry applications. Both types offer advantages in terms of casting quality, durability, and cost-effectiveness in various industrial applications.</p></p>
