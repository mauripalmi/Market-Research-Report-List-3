<p><p>Immunohistochemistry (IHC) testing market includes various types such as Traditional Direct Method, New Direct Method, Enzyme Bridge method, and PAP Method. The Traditional Direct Method involves direct visualization of the antigen-antibody complex. The New Direct Method uses modified antibodies for enhanced specificity. The Enzyme Bridge method utilizes enzymes to amplify the signal. The PAP Method employs a peroxidase-antiperoxidase complex for detection. These different methods offer unique approaches for detecting and studying antigens in tissue samples.</p></p>
