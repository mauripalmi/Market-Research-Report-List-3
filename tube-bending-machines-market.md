<p><p>Tube bending machines are classified into three main types based on their technology: fully electric, hybrid, and hydraulic. Fully electric tube bending machines are powered solely by electricity, offering precise and efficient bending capabilities. Hybrid tube bending machines combine the benefits of both electric and hydraulic systems, providing enhanced flexibility and speed. Hydraulic tube bending machines use hydraulic power to bend tubes, offering high force capabilities for heavy-duty applications. Each type of machine offers unique advantages and is suitable for different types of tube bending tasks.</p></p>
