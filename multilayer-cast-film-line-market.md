<p><p>Multilayer Cast Film Line market is divided into two main types: CPP Film Line and CPE Film Line. CPP Film Line is used for manufacturing of cast polypropylene films, known for their excellent clarity, high tensile strength, and good heat sealability. On the other hand, CPE Film Line is used for producing cast polyethylene films, valued for their superior toughness, flexibility, and impact resistance. Both types of film lines cater to various industries such as food packaging, medical packaging, and industrial applications.</p></p>
