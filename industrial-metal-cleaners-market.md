<p><p>Industrial metal cleaners can be classified into two main types: alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are effective at removing grease, oils, and other organic contaminants from metal surfaces. They are also ideal for cleaning aluminum, copper, and other non-ferrous metals. On the other hand, acid cleaners are better suited for removing rust, scale, and mineral deposits from steel and other ferrous metals. Both types of cleaners are essential for maintaining equipment and machinery in industrial settings.</p></p>
