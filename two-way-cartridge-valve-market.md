<p><ul><li>Intercepting Valve</li><li>Back-Pressure Valve</li><li>Safety Valve</li><li>Regulating Valve</li><li>Splite-flow Valve</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-way cartridge valve market includes several types such as intercepting valve, back-pressure valve, safety valve, regulating valve, splite-flow valve, and others. Intercepting valves are used to stop or allow the flow of fluid in a system. Back-pressure valves help regulate the pressure in a system to prevent damage. Safety valves are crucial for releasing excess pressure to avoid system failures. Regulating valves are used to control the flow rate or pressure in a system. Splite-flow valves separate the flow of fluid into two paths. Others include various types catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
