Fixed Differential Switches: These switches have a set differential pressure range that triggers the switch to turn on or off.</p><p>2. Adjustable Differential Switches: Users can customize the differential pressure range for these switches according to their needs.</p><p>3. Dual-Stage Switches: These switches have two different pressure set points, allowing for more accurate control and monitoring of pressure levels in industrial processes. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in the industrial sector.</p></p>
