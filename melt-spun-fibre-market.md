<p><p>Melt-Spun Fibre Market includes two main types: Filament Melt-Spun Fibers and Staple Melt-Spun Fibers. Filament Melt-Spun Fibers are continuous strands of fibers that are typically used for textiles, ropes, and industrial applications. Staple Melt-Spun Fibers are shorter fibers that are twisted together to form yarns for fabrics and other textile products. Both types of melt-spun fibers are created through a process where a polymer is melted and then extruded through small holes to form fibers. These fibers are known for their strength, durability, and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
